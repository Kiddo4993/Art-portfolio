Ink on Paper

Life After-Effects

Pen & Ink · No Underdrawing

I drew this whole thing freehand using just pen and ink. It's my take on a chaotic aftermath, with figures dropping into a busy, tumbling world. I wanted to pack as many tiny, hidden details into the background as I could.

Gouache & Acrylic

Street Corner

Gouache & Acrylic on Canvas

This is a snapshot of an everyday street corner at dusk, painted with gouache and acrylic. I really wanted to show the contrast you see in the city: some people rushing around with papers flying, while others are just taking a breather.

Silver Award 23rd Canada International Children's Art Festival City of Toronto · August 2025
White Ink on Black Paper

Recurrence Between
Two Best Friends

White Ink & Gouache on Black Paper

I used white ink on black paper to draw a giant wolf made of stars facing a small person. It's meant to show how long-term friendships can feel much bigger than us, about that deep, recurring connection between two best friends over a long time.
