Life After-Effects
Pen & ink on paper, no underdrawing, 2024

I drew this entirely freehand — no pencil sketch underneath. It's about what comes after something big falls apart: angels coming down, people still working, chaos mixed with hope. I wanted every corner to have something happening.

Street Corner
Gouache & acrylic on canvas, 2025

A businessman rushing past an old man sitting on steps — same corner, completely different worlds. I painted the Shanghai skyline in the background because I wanted it to feel like it could be any big city where that contrast exists.

Recurrence Between Two Best Friends
White ink & gouache on black paper, 2025

I drew a giant wolf made of constellation lines leaning down to a small person. It's about a friendship that keeps coming back no matter how much time passes — the kind that feels bigger than you can explain.
